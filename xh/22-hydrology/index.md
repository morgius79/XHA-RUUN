### 22.1.2 Khar-Tor Ven-ghal

Planet-the largest ven-ghal. Khar-Tor range-tor glacier-ak-sha 7,560 ша altitude-the originate-el-en an Serrin Sea-vex empty-el-en. Sha-mor basin-sha Kharak continent-mor ~24%-thar cover-el-en.

**Hydrological regime:**
- Recharge: glacial (45%) + rain (40%) + groundwater (15%)
- High water: Tor-Ven month, discharge up to 69,000 ша³/s
- Low water: Mor-Khal month, discharge 11,300 ша³/s
- Solid runoff: ~194 billion кх/year (anomalously high — volcanic ash-tor)

**Economic significance:**
- 49.6 thous. мо² agricultural land irrigation
- HEP cascade (6 station-ak, total capacity 48 power unit-ak)
- Mouth-tor 427 мо-vex navigable
