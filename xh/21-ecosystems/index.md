## 21.3 Unique Khal-ak

### 21.3.1 Equatorial Jungle-ak

Théxar-the jungle-ak-sha most productive terrestrial biome-sha el-en. Equatorial belt-the (0°–8° latitude) southern Kharak-the an northern Rhuval-the thel-ak-ven.

Structure:

| Layer | Height | Closure | Khal-ak | Photosynthesis |
|-------|--------|---------|---------|----------------|
| A (emergent) | 60–85 m | <10% | 120 | Full khar |
| B (main canopy) | 30–60 m | 85% | 2,800 | Partial rhu-ghal |
| C (understory) | 5–30 m | 60% | 4,200 | Heavy rhu-ghal |
| D (shrub-ak) | 1–5 m | 30% | 3,800 | Low khar |
| E (forest floor) | 0–1 m | <5% | 1,200 | Minimal |

Characteristic sha-ghal — **H₂S khar-filtration**: lower layer-ak-mor thel-ak-sha H₂S-thar khar-tor-the thex absorpt-el-en an metabolism-the khal-el-en-ven — low khar-vex compensation.
