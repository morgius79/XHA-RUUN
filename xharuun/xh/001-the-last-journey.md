# Xha'Ruun-Vokh: Ven-Khal Mor-El (The Last Journey)

> Time: 4 028 Y.E.

---

**Ven-Khal** the-vex en-ak-ven.
*Ven-Khal* balcony-SUP be-PAST-DIR
"Ven-Khal stood on the balcony."

El khar-shak-thar sha-ven en-ak-ven.
He sky-gold-ACC see be-PAST-DIR
"He looked at the golden-bronze sky."

---

**Glossary:**
- the-vex = on the balcony (the = place, -vex = superessive case)
- en-ak-ven = was (en = be, -ak = past, -ven = direct evidence)
- khar-shak-thar = golden-bronze sky (khar = light, shak = bronze, -thar = accusative)
- sha-ven = to see (sha = vision, ven = truth)
