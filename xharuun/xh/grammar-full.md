# Xha'Ruun Grammar — Complete Reference

## 1. Typology
- **Type:** Agglutinative, suffixal
- **Word Order:** SOV (Subject-Object-Verb)
- **Head:** Head-final (modifiers precede nouns, auxiliaries follow verbs)
- **Writing:** Right-to-left, top-to-bottom

## 2. Phonology
- **Consonants:** 24
- **Vowels:** 8 (with length distinction)
- **Syllable:** (C)(C)V(C)(C)
- **Stress:** Dynamic, on first syllable of root

## 4. Syntax

### 4.1 Simple Sentence
SOV: **Xha'ruun-sha the-thar khal-en-ven**
(People-ERG earth-ACC do-PRS-DIR)
"The people work the earth."

### 4.2 Questions
Question particle **-ven?** at the end, or question words:
- Who? **Sha-ven?** (person-truth)
- What? **The-ven?** (thing-truth)
- Where? **The-khar?** (earth-allative)
- When? **Mor-ven?** (time-truth)
- Why? **Ven-thar?** (truth-accusative)
- How? **Khal-ven?** (action-truth)

### 4.3 Complex Sentences
**Conjunctions:**
- and: **rass**
- or: **ven-tor** (truth-from)
- but: **rass-mor** (unity-negation)
- because: **ven-tor** (truth-from)

### 4.4 Relative Clauses
Relative clause precedes the noun, with verb in participial form (-an suffix):
**the-thar khal-an ruun** — "the person who works the earth"
(earth-ACC do-ADJ person)

## 5. Writing System
- **Type:** Mixed syllabary + logograms
- **Direction:** Right-to-left, top-to-bottom
- **Basic syllabary:** 124 signs
- **Logograms:** 48
- **Numerals:** Base-6 (0–5)
- **PUA Unicode:** U+E000-U+E30F
