# Xha'Ruun-Vokh: Vexar Rass-Mor (The Silence of Vexar)

> Time: +2 800 Y.E.

---

**Vexar-sha** the-ven sha-ak-ven.
Vexar-ERG word-DAT say-PAST-DIR
"The Vexar delegate spoke to the Council."

**Rass-Mor** the-ven vex-en-tor.
Silence word-DAT give-PRS-INFR
"The silence gives meaning."

**Glossary:**
- Vexar-sha = Vexar (ergative = the agent/doer)
- the-ven = to the Council (the = council, -ven = dative)
- sha-ak-ven = said (sha = to speak, -ak = past, -ven = direct)
- Rass-Mor = Silence (rass = harmony, mor = time → "timeless harmony")
- vex-en-tor = gives (vex = to give, -en = present, -tor = inferential)
