## 12.1 Sha-Ruun The-El

Xha'Ruun sha-ruun-ak the-ak el en-en-ven: [the-1]-[the-2]. Ruun-thar el-sha-vex en-en-ven.

**Khal-ven-ak:**
- **Khar-Vexar** — "Khar-Vex"
- **Ven-Khal** — "Ven-Xa"
- **Shan-En** — "Shan-En"
