# Xha'Ruun-Vokh: Gharra Rass (The Deep Covenant)

> Time: +2 000 Y.E.

---

**Moryn-ak** gharra-the en-ak-ven.
Moryn-PL depth-INESSIVE be-PAST-DIR
"The Moryn dwell in the depths."

**El-ak** thal-thar rass-en-ven.
They-PL water-ACC harmony-PRS-DIR
"They live in harmony with the water."

**Ven-Khal-sha** el-ak-rass sha-ak-ven.
Ven-Khal-ERG they-PL-COM speak-PAST-DIR
"Ven-Khal spoke with them."

**El-ak-sha** rass-mor vex-ak-ven.
They-PL-ERG silence give-PAST-DIR
"They gave the gift of silence."

**Glossary:**
- Moryn-ak = Moryn (plural)
- gharra-the = in the depths (gharra = deep ocean, -the = inessive)
- thal-thar = water (accusative)
- rass-en-ven = are in harmony (rass = harmony, -en = present, -ven = direct)
- el-ak-rass = with them (el = they, -ak = plural, -rass = comitative)
- rass-mor = silence (harmony + time → "timeless" → silence)
