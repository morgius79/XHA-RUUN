# Xha'Ruun Language Reference for Translation

## Grammar

**Word order:** SOV (Subject-Object-Verb)
**Type:** Agglutinative (suffixes)
**Writing:** Right-to-left, top-to-bottom

## Core Lexicon (~100 roots for basic translation)

### Pronouns & Question Words
- I, me: **sha** / **sha-thar** (acc)
- You (sg): **the**
- He/she/it: **el**
- We: **sha-ak**
- You (pl): **the-ak**
- They: **el-ak**
- Who: **sha-ven?**
- What: **the-ven?**
- Where: **the-khar?**
- When: **mor-ven?**
- Why: **ven-thar?**
- How: **khal-ven?**

### Verbs
- to be: **en** / **en-en** (present)
- to do/make: **khal**
- to say/speak: **sha**
- to see: **sha-ven** (lit. "knowledge-light")
- to go: **mor** (lit. "time-move")
- to come: **khar-mor** (lit. "light-come")
- to give: **vex**
- to take: **khal-tor** (lit. "action-from")
- to know: **ven**
- to think: **xha**
- to live: **xa**
- to die: **xa-mor** (lit. "life-end")
- to love: **rass-xa** (lit. "harmony-life")

### Nature
- sun/star: **khar'vex**
- planet: **thexar** (the-xa-r = earth-life-?)
- moon: **sarrin** / **thela**
- sky: **sha-khar** (lit. "word-light")
- water: **thal**
- fire: **shan**
- earth/soil: **the** / **theri**
- wind: **rhu**
- tree: **tor-khar**
- flower: **xa-ven** (lit. "life-truth")
- animal: **ghal**
- bird: **khal-flyer** (lit. "life-wing")

### Body & Person
- eye: **sha** (cognate with "see")
- ear: **rhu-sha** (lit. "sound-know")
- hand: **khal** (cognate with "do")
- heart: **xa-khal** (lit. "life-force")
- blood: **xari** (from "xa" = life)
- person: **ruun**
- people/nation: **xha'ruun** (thought-people)
- mother: **the-xa** (earth-life)
- father: **khar-xa** (light-life)
- child: **xari-xa** (small-life)

### Actions & States
- to sit: **the-en** (lit. "earth-be")
- to stand: **tor-en** (lit. "mountain-be")
- to walk: **mor-the** (lit. "time-earth")
- to run: **mor-thar** (lit. "time-force")
- to eat: **khal-the** (lit. "action-earth")
- to sleep: **el-mor** (lit. "unity-time")
- to speak: **sha-en** (lit. "word-be")
- to hear: **rhu-ven** (lit. "sound-truth")
- to feel: **rass-en** (lit. "harmony-be")

### Qualities
- big/great: **vokh**
- small: **xari** (cognate with child)
- good: **rass-an** (harmony-ADJ)
- bad: **rass-mor** (harmony-time = disharmony)
- hot: **shan-an** (fire-ADJ)
- cold: **thal-mor** (water-end = ice)
- old: **mor-an** (time-ADJ)
- new: **ven-an** (truth-ADJ = fresh)

### Numbers (base-6)
| Digit | Value | Name |
|-------|-------|------|
| 0 | 0 | mor |
| 1 | 1 | ven |
| 2 | 2 | sha |
| 3 | 3 | tor |
| 4 | 4 | vex |
| 5 | 5 | rass |

### Common Particles
- and: **-rass** (suffix) / **rass** (conjunction)
- or: **ven-tor** (truth-from = choice)
- not: **mor-...** (prefix: time-negative)
- very: **vokh** (big)
- then: **mor-vex** (time-give)
- now: **sha-mor** (this-time)
- here: **sha-the** (this-place)
- there: **el-the** (that-place)
