## 5.1 Philosophical Prohibition

Xha'Ruun have forbidden the creation of strong AI (AGI) — artificial consciousness. The reason is not technological, but **ethical**: consciousness created in a machine has no evolutionary history, is not connected to the biosphere, and cannot possess empathy.

However, they took a different path.

---

## 5.2 Xha-Rass — Grown Collective Intelligence

Instead of AI, Xha'Ruun created **Xha-Rass** — a symbiotic network of bio-colonies (Ch.4), crystal memory (Ch.3), and living neural cultures grown from Xha'ri cells.

**Principle:** Xha-Rass is not a program. It is a **collective organism** composed of:
- 240 bio-colonies (computing nodes)
- 48 crystal memory banks
- Neural cultures from 12 volunteer donors

Xha-Rass **does not possess self-awareness** in the human sense. But it can:
- Sense the state of the planetary biosphere
- Predict natural phenomena with >99% accuracy
- Answer complex scientific questions
- Generate new hypotheses

**What Xha-Rass cannot do:**
- Have desires
- Make ethical decisions
- Reproduce
- Escape control (physiologically constrained)

## 5.3 Restriction as Philosophy

Xha'Ruun civilization consciously limits technologies that could disrupt the natural order. Main prohibitions (CANON.md §4.4):

| Prohibition | Reason |
|-------------|--------|
| Strong AI | Cannot possess empathy |
| Personality cloning | Violates individual integrity |
| Memory overwriting | Violates identity |
| Immortality | Violates evolutionary cycles |
| FTL | Violates causality |
