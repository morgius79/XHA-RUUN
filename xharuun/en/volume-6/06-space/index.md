## 6.1 Xha-Vokh — Living Ships

Xha'Ruun do not build spaceships. They **grow** them.

Xha-Vokh ("Spirit-People") are genetically modified organisms adapted for life in the vacuum of space. Their body is a symbiosis of three components:

1. **Silicon-organic exoskeleton** — protection from radiation and micrometeorites
2. **Photosynthetic membrane** — absorbs starlight for energy
3. **Gas bladder** — internal cavity with atmosphere

| Parameter | Xha-Vokh | Stage of Development |
|-----------|----------|----------------------|
| Wingspan | 12 km | Adult specimen |
| Mass | ~800 t | — |
| Crew | Up to 500 Xha'ri | — |
| Speed | 0.12 c | Cruising |
| Acceleration | 0.02 g | Constant (gravitational resonance) |
| Lifespan | ~1,200 years | — |
| Reproduction | 1 offspring per 80 years | In space |

**Life cycle:** Xha-Vokh are born at orbital "shipyards" — giant nutrient pools where the embryo grows for 12 years. After "birth" (separation from the nutrient medium), a young Xha-Vokh has a wingspan of ~2 km and is used for short voyages. An adult Xha-Vokh (after 200 years) becomes interplanetary.

**Death:** Upon dying, Xha-Vokh return to the atmosphere of Théxar. Their body burns up, but the silicon-organic ash falls to the surface, becoming part of the soil. This is a "closed loop."

## 6.2 Orbital Forest

Around Théxar, at altitudes of 350–1,200 km, grows the **Orbital Forest** — a network of living and semi-living structures forming a permanently inhabited orbital infrastructure.

**Composition:**
- 24 symbiotic stations (living, self-repairing)
- Xha-Vokh on "pasture" (in free flight)
- Crystal reflectors for communication
- GR-resonators for transport

The stations are not sealed in the human sense. They have **bio-membranes** that allow air to pass in only one direction. Pressure is maintained by the organism's own metabolism.

## 6.3 Serrin-Ur and Thel

**Serrin (moon, 425 km):** There are no bases on Serrin — a colony of Xha-Vokh grows there, attached to the surface. It is a "nursery" — a place where young Xha-Vokh spend the first 50 years of life, feeding on regolith.

**Thel (moon, 178 km):** Thel is a source of He-3. Extraction is carried out by symbiotic organisms that filter regolith and release He-3. Not a single building, not a single mechanism — only living structures.

## 6.4 Interstellar Plans

As of 4,028 Y.E.U., Xha'Ruun have not made interstellar flights (the speed of light is absolute, FTL is impossible). However, the **"Seeding"** project is being developed — sending Xha-Vokh embryos to nearby stars in suspended animation, with a "wake" program upon detecting a suitable planet.
