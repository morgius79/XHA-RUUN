## Introduction

The natural resources of Théxar are one of the most important competitive advantages of Xha'Ruun civilization. The elevated metallicity of the protoplanetary cloud and the planet's longer geological history led to the formation of a unique mineral base without analogs in known planetary systems.

The wealth of Théxar's subsurface determined not only the economy but also the history of civilization. The availability of thorium made fusion energy the natural choice for Xha'Ruun; the abundance of metals allowed them to avoid resource conflicts in the early stages of industrialization; and the unique biological resources of the oceans became the foundation of pharmaceuticals and biotechnology.

However, Théxar's resources are distributed unevenly, and this inequality — both between continents and between geological epochs — became an important factor in economic geography and politics.

### 19.2.2 Fusion

Fusion energy is the foundation of Xha'Ruun civilization. Two main cycles:

**Thorium cycle (Th → U-233):**
- Resource base: 10.9 million ру (52% on Kharak)
- Technology: molten salt reactors (MSR)
- Efficiency: ~98% fuel burnup
- Waste: two orders of magnitude less than in alternative cycles
- Electricity cost: 0.8 cents/kWh

**He-3 cycle:**
- Resource base: ~60.6 thousand ру (in the regolith of satellite Thel)
- Technology: magnetic confinement (tokamak)
- Extraction: automated stations on Thel's surface
- Efficiency: direct conversion of nuclear reaction energy into electricity
- Cost: 2.1 cents/kWh (higher due to satellite mining)

### 19.2.3 Orbital Solar Energy

Orbital solar stations (OSS) are the second most important energy source. Located in geostationary orbit (7,764 мо), they transmit energy to the surface via microwave radiation.

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Number of stations | 48 (active) |
| Power (average) | 8.75 GW/station |
| Transfer efficiency | 78% |
| Area of one station | 0.5 мо² |
| Capacity factor | 0.92 (high due to absence of night in orbit) |

### 19.2.4 Geothermal and Hydroenergy

Geothermal stations are located in tectonically active zones — the Khar-Tor range (Kharak) and the Irkhen chain (Rhuval). Total capacity — 120 GW.

Hydroenergy is fully developed: all major rivers have cascades of hydroelectric plants. Total capacity — 240 GW. The largest HEP is **Thar-1** on the Thar River (capacity 22 GW).

## 19.5 Resources and Ecology

### 19.5.1 Sustainable Development

Xha'Ruun civilization has achieved a high level of ecological sustainability through:

1. **Fusion energy** — minimal emissions and waste
2. **Complete recycling** — >95% of industrial waste is recycled
3. **Population regulation** — the Commandment of Growth (CANON.md §4.3)
4. **Protected zones** — 15% of the planet declared Zones of Silence

### 19.5.2 Limitations and Deficits

Despite overall wealth, there are limitations:

| Resource | Status | Reason |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Arable land | Limited deficit | 12% of land suitable for agriculture |
| Fresh water (regions) | Local deficit | Shan Desert, highlands |
| Gold (Au) | Moderate deficit | High demand in electronics |
| He-3 | Sufficient | Mining on Thel — rising costs |
