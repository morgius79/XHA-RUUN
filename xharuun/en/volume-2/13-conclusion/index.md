## Key Lessons of History

1. **Technology does not solve all problems** — The First Empire possessed bronze, but fell due to ecological folly.
2. **Knowledge is fragile** — It took 1,400 years of Dark Ages to recover what was lost.
3. **Unity is a choice** — 8 regions could have continued war, but chose peace.
4. **Progress is not linear** — civilization has passed through rises and falls.
