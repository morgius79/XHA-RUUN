## 6.1 Founding of the Unity Council (+850)

+850 civ. — 49 representatives (by number of guilds and regions) gathered in Khar-Vexar to create the supreme governing body.

### 6.1.1 Structure of the Council

| Component | Composition | Function |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Head of Council | Elected for 10 years | Presidency |
| Chamber of Guilds | 24 members (2 per guild) | Economy, science |
| Chamber of Regions | 24 members (3 per domain) | Territory, population |
| Keeper of Knowledge | 1 (lifelong) | Canon oversight |

### 6.1.2 First Decisions

1. Adoption of the Unified Calendar (0 E.U.)
2. Creation of a Unified Writing System
3. Founding of the Guilds (12)
4. Construction of the orbital elevator

## 6.4 Technological Progress by Era

### Industrial Era (+1,000 – +1,800)

Steam, coal, steel. Construction of railways. Electrification.

### Atomic Era (+1,200 – +2,000)

Thermonuclear fusion, orbital elevator. End of fossil fuels.

### Information Era (+1,800 – +2,800)

Bio-optical computing, quantum communication. Artificial intelligence (limited).

### Era of Abundance (+2,800 – present)

Post-scarcity economy, full automation. Colonization of moons.
