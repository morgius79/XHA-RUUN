## 8.1 Origin of the Guilds

The guild system originated as early as the First Empire as associations of craftsmen by profession. After the fall of the Empire (−2,150), guilds became centers for the preservation of knowledge and professional skills.

## 8.2 Formation of the System

| Period | Development |
|--------|----------|
| −2,000 – −2,150 | Craft associations (First Empire) |
| −2,150 – −750 | Guilds as centers of knowledge |
| −750 – +850 | Guilds as economic unions |
| +850 – +1,500 | Formal recognition by the Unity Council |
| +1,500 – present | Modern structure (12 guilds) |
