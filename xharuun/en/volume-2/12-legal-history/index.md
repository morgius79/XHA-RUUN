## 12.2 The Imperial Code

Created by Verksaren I (−6,150). 48 articles.

Key principles:

- Equal for equal (restitution, not vengeance)
- Community responsibility
- Inviolability of envoys
- Right of a slave to buy freedom
- Freedom of slaves' children
