## 14.1 Xha'ri — Dominant Species

Xha'ri — the first sapient species of Théxar to achieve civilization. Their history is described in previous chapters.

## 14.2 Kel'vash — Silicon-Organic Species

### 14.2.1 Habitat

Kel'vash inhabit highlands and deserts — extreme environments with low humidity and high radiation. Their biochemistry is based on silicon-organic compounds.

### 14.2.2 Discovery and Contact

| Event | Year |
|-------|------|
| First evidence (cave paintings) | ~−8,000 |
| First confirmed contact | −2,400 |
| Acknowledgment of sapience | −2,200 |
| Joining the Council of Unity | +1,200 |

### 14.2.3 Role in Civilization

Kel'vash specialize as engineers and builders. Their contributions:

- Tunnels and underground structures
- Mining
- Architecture in extreme conditions

## 14.3 Moryn — Sapient Ocean Dwellers

### 14.3.1 Habitat

Moryn inhabit the deep-water zones of the Gharra ocean (depths 500–3,000 m). Their civilization is built on bioluminescent communication.

### 14.3.2 Discovery and Contact

| Event | Year |
|-------|------|
| First observations (from boats) | ~−3,000 |
| Confirmed contact | −1,200 |
| Establishment of permanent communication | −800 |
| Joining the Council of Unity | +1,500 |

### 14.3.3 Communication Problems

- Language barrier (sound vs. bioluminescence)
- Different perception of time (Moryn metabolism 3× slower)
- Different ethical systems

## 14.4 The Principle of Three

The Council of Unity is based on the principle of three: no decision is made without consultation of all three species (Law of Consent, CANON.md §4.3).
