## 11.2 History of Formation

Domain borders reflect historical and cultural differences:

- Kharak-Thar — the oldest domain, core of the First Empire
- Rhuval-Ven — center of resistance to the Empire
- Vexar-Shan — isolated, joined later than all others
- Polar — research stations, no permanent population
