## 17.1 Calendar development

| Period | Calendar | Basis |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| −10,000 – −8,000 | Lunar (Serrin) | Moon phases |
| −8,000 – −5,000 | Lunisolar | Serrin + solstices |
| −5,000 – −2,150 | Imperial | 14 months |
| −2,150 – −750 | Regional | Various systems |
| −750 – +850 | Pre-Unified | 14 months + 5 days |
| +850 – present | Unified Calendar | Standard |

## 17.2 Unified Calendar

Adopted in year 0 U.E.:

- 14 months of 29.5 days = 413 days
- 5 additional Renovation days = 418 days
- Correction: every 5th year — 6 Renovation days

Accuracy: 1 day error per 3,200 years.
