## 6.1 Pantheon (Historical)

Early beliefs included a pantheon of deities personifying forces of nature.

| Gharra | Depth, mysteries | Spiral | −8,000 – −2,000 |

## 6.2 Modern Religious Thought

Modern Xha'Ruun society is predominantly secular. Religion exists as a philosophical tradition (School of the "Three Paths"). ~15% of the population identifies with some form of spiritual practice.
