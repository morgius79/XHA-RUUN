## 1.1 Visual Art

Xha'Ruun visual art traditions span 12,000 years — from the cave paintings of Ven-Shar to digital installations.

### 1.1.1 Periods

| Period | Character | Examples |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Cave (−12,000 – −6,000) | Ochre, charcoal, animals | Ven-Shar (Vexar) |
| Imperial (−6,000 – −2,150) | Frescoes, mosaic, basalt | Verksan palaces |
| Crisis (−2,150 – −750) | Ascetic, religious | Thar temples |
| Renaissance (−750 – +850) | Realism, perspective | Portraits, landscapes |
| Modern (+850 – present) | Digital, interactive | Holograms, bio-art |

## 1.2 Color Palette

Traditional Xha'Ruun art colors reflect the natural hues of the planet:

- Gold (#c9a84c) — light of Khar'Vex
- Azure (#5b7db5) — sky of Théxar
- Bronze (#8b6f4e) — earth
- Red — life, blood
- Purple — night, Serrin
