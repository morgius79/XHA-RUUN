## 4.1 Discovery

The first subglacial lake was discovered in +2,800 y. beneath the southern polar shield. By 4,028 y., 4 major lakes are known.

## 4.3 Ecosystem

100% endemics. H₂S + Fe²⁺ chemosynthesis. Microorganisms isolated for millions of cycles represent "living fossils."
