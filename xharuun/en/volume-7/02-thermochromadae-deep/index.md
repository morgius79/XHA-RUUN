## 2.1 Evolutionary Radiation

The class Thermochromadae emerged ~350 million cycles ago. Since then, it has produced ~24,000 described species — from 0.2 ша to 10 ша in height.

### 2.1.1 Key Adaptations

| Adaptation | Function | Evolutionary Advantage |
|------------|----------|------------------------|
| Hexapodalism | 6 limbs, functional division | Locomotion + manipulation |
| Dual bloodstream | 2 hearts: thoracic + abdominal | Independence of breathing and movement |
| Trinocular vision | 3 eyes | Depth accuracy 0.02° |
| Thermochromatism | Skin thermoregulation | Adaptation to diurnal cycles |
| 6-nucleotide code | 216 codons | Radiation resistance |
