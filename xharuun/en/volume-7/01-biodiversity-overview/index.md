## 1.1 Scale of Biodiversity

Théxar is a planet of exceptional biological diversity. Over 4.2 billion cycles of evolution under an orange sun, active tectonics, and abundant sulfur have shaped a biosphere without parallel in the known part of the galaxy.

## 1.2 Unique Features of the Biosphere

### 1.2.1 Six-Letter Genetic Code

The fundamental difference of Théxar's biosphere is 6 nucleotides (G, C, A, T, P, X) instead of 4.

**Consequences:**
- 216 codons (instead of 64)
- 24 proteinogenic amino acids (instead of 20)
- 5× higher genome noise immunity
- Protein complexity 30–40% higher

### 1.2.2 Sulfur Cycle

H₂S is not a toxin but a fully functional metabolic substrate. H₂S chemosynthesis is as widespread as photosynthesis.

### 1.2.3 D-Chirality

Amino acids are D-forms, sugars are L-forms. The opposite chirality means that Earth organisms cannot interact with Théxar's biosphere at the molecular level.
