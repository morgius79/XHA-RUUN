## 5.1 Scale

Théxar's karst systems are the planet's largest underground ecosystems.

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Total explored length | ~340,000 мо |
| Area of underground ecosystems | ~0.8 million мо² |
| Largest cave | Thal-Ven (2,840 мо depth) |
| Endemism | ~95% |

## 5.2 Cave Energetics

In the absence of light, primary production is provided by H₂S and Fe²⁺ chemosynthesis.
