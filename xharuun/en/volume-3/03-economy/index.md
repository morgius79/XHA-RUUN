## 3.1 Post-Scarcity Economy

The Xha'Ruun economy is post-scarcity. Basic resources are available to everyone free of charge.

| Resource | Status | Provision |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Energy | Free | Fusion + Khar'Vex |
| Food | Free (basic) | Automated agriculture |
| Housing | Free (basic) | Autonomous modules |
| Medicine | Free | Guild of Medics |
| Education | Free | Guild of Scholars |

## 3.2 Guild Economy

72% of GDP is controlled by 12 Guilds. The remaining 28% consists of independent producers and the service sector.

| Indicator | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Planetary GDP | 124 trillion s.u. |
| GDP per capita | 44,000 s.u. |
| Guild share of GDP | 72% |
| Growth | 2.8%/year |
| Unemployment | 2.8% |
