## 6.1 Formal Structure

Xha'Ruun society is formally casteless. Social status is determined by:

| Factor | Weight | Comment |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Guild | 40% | Membership and rank |
| Merits | 30% | Contribution to society |
| Education | 20% | Level of knowledge |
| Lineage | 10% | Family (minimal influence) |

## 6.2 Social Mobility

High mobility: 60% of guild members come from families of a different status.

| Path | Difficulty | Time |
|------|-----------|------|
| Education → Guild | Low | 10–15 years |
| Merits → Recognition | Medium | 5–20 years |
| Change of guild | Medium | 1–3 years |
| Entrepreneurship | Low | Any |
