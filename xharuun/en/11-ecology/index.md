## Introduction

The biosphere of Théxar is one of the richest in the known part of the galaxy. An estimated 8 to 12 million species exist on the planet, of which only ~2.8 million have been described (~28%).

Six limbs are the dominant body plan for large animals. However, Théxar's evolution also produced other solutions: four- and eight-limbed forms, radially symmetric organisms, and pseudopodial giants.

This chapter is an overview of Théxar's living world: from microscopic bacteria to giant ocean filter-feeders, from deserts to ice caps.

## 11.2 Microorganisms and Protozoa

### 11.2.1 Bacteria and Archaea

Théxar's bacteria are the foundation of all ecosystems. They participate in key biogeochemical cycles.

| Group | Energy | Environment | Function |
|-------|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Phototrophs** | Light | Surface | O₂ production |
| **Chemolithotrophs** | H₂S → S⁰ | Hydrothermal vents, depths | Sulfur cycle |
| **Methylotrophs** | CH₄ | Anaerobic zones | Carbon cycle |
| **Silicon bacteria** | Si-compounds | Soils | Soil formation |

**Unique group — silicon bacteria (Silobacteriae):** use silicon in the cell wall, creating microscopic glass shells. They are the primary soil-formers on Théxar.

### 11.2.2 Protozoa (Xa'xhal — "living dust")

Théxar's unicellular organisms include flagellates, amoeboid, and ciliate forms. A distinctive feature is the presence of **Si-channels** in the membrane of ~60% of species, providing more efficient ion transport.

## 11.3 Plant Life (Xa-The — "life of the earth")

### 11.3.1 General Characteristics

Théxar's flora is adapted to the Khar'Vex spectrum (→ see [Ch.02, "Khar'Vex"]). The dominant photosynthetic pigment is **chromophyll** (absorption peak 570 nm).

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Dominant pigment | Chromophyll a+b |
| Absorption peak | ~570 nm |
| Foliage color | Blue-violet → silvery |
| Average tree height | 73–98 ша (jungle) |
| Max height | **173 ша** (Six-winged Oak) |
| Annual rings | **6 layers** per year |

> **KEY FACT**
>
> Théxar's foliage has a **blue-violet and silvery hue**, not green. This is a direct consequence of the star's spectrum: chromophyll absorbs orange light (570 nm) and reflects blue-violet. The Xha'Ruun eye sees these colors as tones of "living silver."

## 11.4 Animal Life

### 11.4.1 Class Thermochromadae — Dominant Taxon

The class **Thermochromadae** ("warm-colored") — an endemic vertebrate class of Théxar, to which Xha'Ruun belong (→ see [Ch.05, "Species Xha'Ruun"]). Includes ~24,000 species — from small reptile-like (0.24 ша) to giant herbivores (9.8 ша).

**Common characteristics of Thermochromadae:**
- Hexapodal symmetry (6 limbs)
- Chitinous-bony skeleton
- Double circulatory system (2 hearts)
- Cutaneous thermoregulation through chromatophores
- Cold-blooded (basal) / warm-blooded (higher taxa)

#### Deep-sea Communities

- **Blind crabs** (8 limbs, chemoreception)
- **Bioluminescent jellyfish** (blue-green light through xanthosine)

## 11.6 Unique Ecosystems

### 11.6.1 Equatorial Jungles

Cover ~10% of the planet's surface. Characterized by:

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Biomass | ~2,545.5 ру/мо² |
| Canopy height | 73–98 ша |
| Number of layers | **6** |
| Species/мо² | ~8,000 |
| H₂S in ground layer | 0.002% |

**Six-layer forest structure:**
1. **Emergent** (>98 ша) — giants (Six-winged Oak)
2. **Upper canopy** (49–98 ша) — main crowns
3. **Middle canopy** (18–49 ша) — lianas, epiphytes
4. **Lower canopy** (6–18 ша) — shade-tolerant
5. **Understory** (1.2–6.1 ша) — shrubs
6. **Forest floor** (0–1.2 ша) — mosses, ferns, bacteria

### 11.6.2 Cave Ecosystems

Karst formations of Kharak and Vexar contain isolated underground ecosystems (total area ~9,914 мо²). Some caves have been isolated from the surface for millions of cycles.

| Characteristic | Adaptation |
|----------------|------------|
| Light | Absent → blindness, loss of pigmentation |
| Energy | H₂S chemosynthesis (90%), guano (10%) |
| Species size | On average smaller than terrestrial |
| Endemism | ~95% of species are cave endemics |
| Lifespan | 3–5 times longer than terrestrial |

**Characteristic species:**
- **Blind Six-legged Crab** (Xhel-kreb) — colorless, 17 па
- **Cave Filter Worm** (Xhel-thal) — 0.61–3.66 ша
- **Bioluminescent Moss** (Xhel-khar) — faint blue glow

## 11.7 Extinctions and Paleontology

### 11.7.1 Mass Extinctions

In Théxar's history, **5 major mass extinctions** have been recorded:

| Event | Time (million cycles ago) | Cause | Species Lost |
|-------|-----------------|---------|--------------|
| **1st** (Oxygen) | 3,000 | Atmosphere saturation with O₂ from photosynthesis | ~40% of anaerobes |
| **2nd** (Volcanic) | 1,800 | Supereruption, 800-year winter | ~55% |
| **3rd** (Glacial) | 900 | Complete glaciation | ~65% |
| **4th** (Impact) | 250 | Asteroid 3.05 мо (Gharra crater) | ~70% |
| **5th** (Crisis) | ~0.0021 | Anthropogenic (Xha'Ruun Crisis Era) | ~12% |

### 11.7.2 Impact Event −250 Million Cycles

The largest extinction — an asteroid impact 3.05 мо in diameter in the Gharra ocean (250 million cycles ago). The crater (65 мо in diameter) is now a marine basin.

Consequences:
- Tsunami up to 2,440 ша high
- Winter lasting 15 years
- Extinction of ~70% of species
- Vacated ecological niches → accelerated evolution of Xha'Ruun ancestors

## 11.8 Xha'Ruun and Ecology

### 11.8.1 Ecological Ethics

The Xha'Ruun attitude toward nature is shaped by historical experience (→ see [Ch.07, "Crisis Era"]):

1. **The "six-step" principle** — before any large-scale intervention in nature, consequences are assessed for 6 generations ahead
2. **Species reserves** — 12% of the planet's area is protected
3. **Asteroid mining** — metals are mined in space, not on the planet's surface
4. **Reintroduction** — successful restoration of 8 species exterminated during the Crisis Era

### 11.8.2 Economics and Ecology

| Parameter | Value | Note |

| CO₂ emissions | ~0 (net) | Fusion + recycling |

| Extinctions (modern) | 0 since +2,100 Y.E. | Complete cessation |
