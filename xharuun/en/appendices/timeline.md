# Appendix 1: Consolidated Timeline of the Xha'Ruun Civilization

> *"Time is a river. A timeline is a map of the river."*

---

**Legend:**
- **y. civ.** — year of civilization (Xha'Ruun calendar)
- **y. E.U.** — year of the Era of Unity (+850 y. civ.)
