# Appendix 2: Glossary of Terms

Complete glossary of terms from the Xha'Ruun encyclopedia. Format: **term** [language] — definition. → see corresponding chapter for context.

---

## A–Z (Latin)

**Khar'Vex** [Xha'Ruun] — star of the system, orange dwarf K2V. "Light-Giver." → see [Ch.02]

**Kharak** [Xha'Ruun] — largest continent of Théxar (1.41 million мо²). "Edge of Light." → see [Ch.03]

**Rhuval** [Xha'Ruun] — second largest continent of Théxar (0.90 million мо²). "Great Land." → see [Ch.03]

**Vexar** [Xha'Ruun] — third continent of Théxar (0.76 million мо²). "Creating Land." → see [Ch.03]

**Sha'Ruun** [Xha'Ruun] — the Xha'Ruun language. "Language of the People." → see [Ch.06]

**Théxar** [Xha'Ruun] — planet, homeworld of the Xha'Ruun. "Progenitor World." → see [Ch.03]

**Serrin** [Xha'Ruun] — the largest satellite of Théxar (R=86 мо). → see [Ch.03]

**Thel** [Xha'Ruun] — the small satellite of Théxar (R=36 мо). → see [Ch.03]

**Thalass** [Xha'Ruun] — the largest moon of Rhuun (R=577 мо). Candidate for colonization. → see [Ch.02]

**Xha'Ruun** [Xha'Ruun] — sentient species. "Thinking People" (full form: Xha'Ruun-Vokh). → see [Ch.05]

## E

**Emotional resonance** [psych.] — group synchronization of emotions through the pheromone channel. → see [Ch.05]

**Electroreception** [biol.] — rudimentary ability of Xha'Ruun to sense weak electric fields (~8 µV/ша). → see [Ch.05]

## F

**Pheromone communication** [biol.] — transmission of emotional information through 8 types of pheromones. → see [Ch.05]

**Photosystem III** [biochem.] — additional photosynthetic complex, absorbing in UV-A. → see [Ch.04]

**First Library** [hist.] — the largest collection of knowledge from the Era of Flourishing (founded −4,800 y.). → see [Ch.07]

**First Voice** [hist., pol.] — title of the ruler of the First Empire; now — Chairman of the Council of Unity. → see [Ch.07]

## G

---

## H

**Heliothermy** [biol.] — the ability of Xha'Ruun to absorb IR radiation through the skin for thermoregulation. → see [Ch.05]

**Hemocyanin** [biochem.] — copper-containing pigment of Xha'Ruun blood, giving it a blue-green color. → see [Ch.05]

---

## I

**Infrasound sensitivity** [biol.] — the ability of Xha'Ruun to hear 8–20 Hz. Ancestral heritage for detecting seismic activity. → see [Ch.05]

---

## K

**Kharru** [biol.] — domesticated herbivore (large livestock). Six-legged, class Thermochromadae. → see [Ch.05]

**Kharinium (Kh)** [chem.] — superheavy element (Z=127, A=327). Ferromagnet, superconductor. Half-life: 12,400 years. → see [Ch.09]

**Khar Cloud** [astr.] — outer region of the Khar'Vex system (30–800 AU), cloud of outer bodies. → see [Ch.02]

**Kvantrophone** [tech.] — musical instrument that creates sound via quantum fluctuations in a superconductor. → see [Ch.08]

**Quartet hearing** [biol.] — ability to determine sound direction with ±0.5° accuracy thanks to 4 ears. → see [Ch.05]

**Red Rot** [hist.] — epidemic of −2,120 – −2,105 y. civ. Killed ~200 million Xha'Ruun. → see [Ch.07]

---

## M

**Maglev** [tech.] — high-speed magnetic levitation train. Up to 4,400 ша/миг. → see [Ch.09]

**Meritocracy** [soc.] — social system where status is determined by merit, not birth. → see [Ch.08]
