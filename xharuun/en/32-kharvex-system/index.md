### 32.2.2 Vax

The closest planet to Khar'Vex. A scorching rocky world with a surface temperature of +266.4°X. The absence of an atmosphere is a consequence of proximity to the star. The surface is pockmarked with craters and solidified basalt flows.

### 32.2.3 Xhel

A greenhouse planet with a dense CO₂ atmosphere (pressure 90 tyazh). Surface temperature +157.6°X. Has one moon — **Xhel-Mor** (radius 24.4 mo).
