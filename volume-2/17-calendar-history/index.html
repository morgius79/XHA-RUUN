17.1 Calendar Development

Period Calendar Basis
−10,000 – −8,000 Lunar (Serrin) Satellite phases
−8,000 – −5,000 Solar-lunar Serrin + solstices
−5,000 – −2,150 Imperial 14 months
−2,150 – −750 Regional Different systems
−750 – +850 Pre-Unity 14 months + 5 days
+850 – present Unified Calendar Standard

17.2 Unified Calendar

Adopted in 0 Y.U.:

  • 14 months of 29.5 days = 413 days
  • 5 additional Renewal days = 418 days
  • Correction: every 5th year — 6 Renewal days

Accuracy: 1 day of error per 3,200 years.
