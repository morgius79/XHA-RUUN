Volume II: History — Chapter 5: The Renaissance and the Second Unity

"From the ashes of the First Empire, what rose were not empires — what rose were ideas. Freedom, equality, knowledge. And these ideas proved stronger than any army." — From the "Manifesto of the Renaissance," −600

Related sections: → see [Volume II, Ch.4] Era: −750 – +850 (1,600 years) Length: 35 pages

5.1 The Unity Treaty (−750)

The key event of the era. Representatives of 8 regions of Kharak gathered in the ancient capital to sign the Unity Treaty.

Terms of the treaty:

  1. Free trade between the 8 regions
  2. Unified system of weights and measures
  3. Freedom of movement for citizens
  4. Renunciation of war between regions
  5. Joint defense against external threats
  6. Unified calendar (adopted in year 0)

5.2 Cultural Renaissance

−600 – −200 — a period of cultural flowering known as the "Age of Enlightenment."

5.2.1 Philosophy

The School of the Three Paths — a philosophical movement that formulated the foundations of Xha'Ruun ethics:

  • The Path of Knowledge (Ven) — truth through science
  • The Path of Harmony (Rass) — balance with nature
  • The Path of Action (Khal) — transformation of the world

5.3.2 Electrification

−240 — the discovery of electricity and creation of the first generator. By +200, electricity had become the primary energy source for industry.

5.4 Unification of the Continents

5.4.1 Kharak — Rhuval

After the Unity Treaty, Kharak and Rhuval established permanent diplomatic and trade relations. By −200, Rhuval had joined the treaty.

5.4.2 Vexar

Vexar, due to its geographical isolation, joined later — by +200, after the establishment of regular maritime communication across the Gharra Ocean.

5.5 Prerequisites for the Unity Council

By +500, it became clear that the regional system of governance could not cope with the growing complexity of the intercontinental economy.

5.5.1 Problems

  • Different tax systems across regions
  • Trade disputes between guilds
  • Lack of a unified labor code

5.5.2 Solution

+800 — the beginning of negotiations on the creation of a central governing body. +850 — the founding of the Unity Council.

Chapter Summary

  1. The Unity Treaty (−750) united 8 regions of Kharak.
  2. The Age of Enlightenment (−600 – −200) — flourishing of philosophy and science.
  3. The School of the Three Paths — the foundation of Xha'Ruun ethics (Knowledge, Harmony, Action).
  4. Technological breakthroughs: compass, electricity, telegraph, orbital flight.
  5. Vexar joined the Unity by +200.
  6. The founding of the Unity Council (+850) completed the era.
