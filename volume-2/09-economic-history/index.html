9.2 Post-Scarcity Economy

The modern Xha'Ruun economy is post-scarcity:

  • Basic resources (energy, food, housing) — free
  • Handcrafted goods and art — market-based
  • Labor — voluntary (guild-based)
  • Planetary GDP: 124 trillion solar units
  • GDP per capita: 44,000 s.u.
