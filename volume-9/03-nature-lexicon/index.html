Volume IX: Dictionary — Chapter 3: Nature — Lexicon

"To name is to understand. In every word about nature among the Xha'Ruun lies the observation of thousands of cycles." — Natural History, Academy

3.1 Heavens and Cosmos

Xha'Ruun Translation Etymology
khar'vex star, luminary khar (light) + vex (to give)
thexar planet the (soil) + xa (life) + ar (bearer)
sarrin satellite, moon from sar (wanderer)
thela captured body from thel (captive)

3.2 Elements

Xha'Ruun Translation Etymology
thalas ocean, northern waters thal (depth)
gharra southern waters, abyss ghar (bottom)
kharak northern continent khar (light) + ak (land)
rhuval southern continent rhuu (people) + val (strength)
vexar eastern continent vex (to create) + ar

3.3 Flora and Fauna

Xha'Ruun Translation
xa'the plant (life of the earth)
tor'khar sky tree (giant)
kharra kharru (domesticated animal)
ghal'shar plains runner
