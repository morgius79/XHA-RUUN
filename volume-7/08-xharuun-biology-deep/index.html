"Three sexes, two hearts, four lungs — nature does not create excess. In every organ — millions of cycles of evolution, in every cycle — an answer to the challenge of the environment." — Anatomical Treatise, Academy

8.1 Hexapodalism

6 limbs: 2 lower (locomotion), 2 middle (primary manipulators), 2 upper (wing-manipulators). Lower — three-toed. Middle — four-toed. Upper — three-toed with claws.

8.2 Three Sexes

Sex Symbol Proportion Function
Male ♂ 15% Genetic material
Female ♀ 20% Eggs, gestation
Neuter ⚲ 65% Care, labor, social structure
