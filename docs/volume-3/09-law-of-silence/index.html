"Silence is not the absence of sound. Silence is the absence of interference. There are places where nature must be left to itself." — From the Law of Silence, CANON.md §4.3

9.1 The Law

The Law of Silence is one of the six fundamental laws of the Union. Certain zones of the planet are declared technology-free reserves.
