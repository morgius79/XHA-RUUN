Volume VI: Technology — Chapter 3: Xha-Ven — The Biosphere Network

"Electricity is a crude, primitive way to transmit information. Thousands of years of evolution created the perfect network — mycelium. We simply learned to speak its language." — First Keeper of the Network, +2,800 Y.E.

Related sections: → see [Volume VI, Ch.1] Length: 30 pages

3.1 Living Network (Xha-Ven)

Xha'Ruun do not use electromagnetic waves for communication. Instead, they created the Xha-Ven biosphere network — a planetary mycelial organism that permeates the crust of Théxar.

How it works:

Deep beneath the planet's surface (at depths of 500–2,000 m), a network of modified mycelia (Mycophyta) has grown, connected into a single organism. This organism — Rass-Mor (the Unified Consciousness) — is not sentient, but serves as an ideal transmission medium.

Parameter Xha-Ven Terrestrial Analog
Transmission speed Instantaneous (quantum entanglement in mycelium) —
Bandwidth ~10²⁴ bit/s (theoretically infinite) —
Coverage 100% land, 60% ocean (surface) —
Security Absolute (data is part of the organism) —
Energy consumption Powered by geothermal heat —
Network size ~12 billion km of hyphae —

Principle: The hyphae of the mycelium contain chains of molecules in a state of quantum entanglement. Information is encoded not by a signal, but by the state of entanglement. A change in one part of the network is instantly reflected in all others — regardless of distance.

3.2 Crystal Memory (Ven-Khal)

Information is not stored on disks or in the "cloud." It is stored in the lattice defects of specially grown silicon-carbide crystals.

Parameter Ven-Khal crystal Terrestrial Analog
Capacity 10¹⁸ TB (crystal 1 cm³) —
Durability >10⁹ years (at room T) —
Read energy 0 (optical reading) —
Record destruction Only possible via nuclear decay —

How it looks: Xha'Ruun libraries are halls with shelves of transparent, fist-sized crystals. To "read" a crystal, it is placed in a resonator that passes the light of an H₂S laser through it. Light passing through the lattice defects is distorted — and information is reconstructed from these distortions.

A single 1 cm³ crystal holds all knowledge ever produced by humanity — repeated 10,000 times.

3.3 Emotional Transmission (Sha-Ven)

Xha'Ruun have developed a technology that transmits not just information, but sensory-emotional experience. This is not a "video call." It is total immersion into the consciousness of another being.

Principle: A Ven-Khal crystal can record not only data but also neural patterns. During playback, the recipient experiences the recorded experience — seeing, hearing, and feeling the same as the source.

Applications:

  • Education: not reading a textbook, but living through the experience of a great engineer
  • Diplomacy: conveying one's worldview to another species
  • Art: sharing the inner world of an artist
  • Memory: preserving the personality of the deceased (but not restoring it — a philosophical prohibition)

3.4 Moryn Language — Without Technology

Moryn do not need Xha-Ven. They communicate through bioluminescent patterns visible at depths of up to 3,000 m. Their "speech" is color and rhythm. A single flash can convey information equivalent to 10,000 words.

For translation between species, living translators are used — modified organisms that symbiotize with both Moryn and Xha'ri, conveying meaning in both directions.
