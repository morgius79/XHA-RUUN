Chapter 34: Measurement Systems of Théxar

"Everything has its measure. Distance — the length of a kharru step. Time — the beating of two hearts. Matter — the weight of water in cupped hands. To measure is to understand." — From the "Book of Measures," canonical text, Era of Unity
