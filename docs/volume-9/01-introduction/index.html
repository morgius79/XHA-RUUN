Volume IX: Dictionary — Chapter 1: Introduction to the Lexicon

"A word is not just a sound. A word is a key to reality. To know the name of a thing is to have power over it." — From the "Book of Names," Archive of the First Library

1.1 Structure of the Dictionary

The Xha'Ruun lexicon is organized by thematic sections. Each entry contains:

  • Root/word — in Xha'Ruun (transliteration)
  • Part of speech — root, noun, verb, adjective
  • Translation — into common usage
  • Etymology — origin (if known)
  • Example — usage in context
