Volume VII: Nature — Chapter 5: Cave Ecosystems

"Below, beneath the Central Plain, there is a world where there is no day or night. Where rivers flow in total darkness for millions of cycles. And where life has forgotten what light looks like." — Speleologist Khal-Ven, +3,500 y.

Related sections: → see [Ch.21, "Ecosystems"] Volume: 25 pages

5.1 Scale

Théxar's karst systems are the planet's largest underground ecosystems.

Parameter Value
Total explored length ~340,000 мо
Area of underground ecosystems ~0.8 million мо²
Largest cave Thal-Ven (2,840 мо depth)
Endemism ~95%

5.2 Cave Energetics

In the absence of light, primary production is provided by H₂S and Fe²⁺ chemosynthesis.
