Ven-Mor Vex-Ven

The Last Journey

From the Xha'Ruun archive. Time: 4028 E.E. Translated from the Russian original.

I. The Balcony

Ven-Khal Khar-Vexar-thexar-tor sha-ven en-ak-ven.

Ven-Khal Khar-Vexar-planet-from look be-PAST-DIRECT

Ven-Khal looked upon the planet Khar-Vexar.

The mor-vex the-mor-ven thex en-ak-ven. Ven-Khal-sha the-thar sha-ven en-ak-ven.

The time-IN earth-time-DAT stand be-PAST-DIRECT. Ven-Khal-ERG earth-ACC see be-PAST-DIRECT

He stood in the moment between evening and earth. Ven-Khal saw the land below.

II. The Council

"Khal-ak-thex-sha mor-khal-en-en-ven," Ven-Khal-sha en-ak-ven.

"Life-PL-beat-ERG NEG-do-PRES-DIRECT," Ven-Khal-ERG say-PAST-DIRECT

"My hearts are losing their rhythm," said Ven-Khal.

"Khal-ak-mor-sha the-vex en-ak-ven — 62 ven ghor-ven, 48 ven thal-ven."

"Life-PL-time-ERG earth-SUP be-PAST-DIRECT — 62 TRUTH chest-DAT, 48 TRUTH abdomen-DAT"

"The readings stand: 62 in the chest, 48 in the abdomen."

"Thex-ven Khal-en-ven?" Ven-El-sha en-ak-ven.

"Where-DAT go-PRES-DIRECT?" Council-ERG say-PAST-DIRECT

"Where will you go?" asked the Council Leader.

"Thar-thar-the ven. Thex-en-khal mor en-ak-ven."

"Thar-valley-IN truth. Journey-begin time be-PAST-DIRECT"

"To the Valley of Thar. Where it all began. The time has come."

III. The Journey

Ven-Khal GR-khorridor-thar khal-ak-ven — 3 thex-mor thexen en-ak-ven.

Ven-Khal GR-corridor-ACC do-PAST-DIRECT — 3 day-journey long be-PAST-DIRECT

Ven-Khal took the GR-corridor — a journey of three days.

Thar-thar Ven-Khal-thar thek-rass en-ak-ven.

Thar-valley Ven-Khal-ACC silence-hug be-PAST-DIRECT

The Valley of Thar embraced Ven-Khal with silence.

Thal-sha thal-vex thex-en-ak-ven, 9000-gharr tor en-ak-ven.

Water-ERG water-SUP carry-be-PAST-DIRECT, 9000-cycle force be-PAST-DIRECT

The river carried upon itself, as it had for 9000 cycles.

Vex-Mor — khal-sarrin thex — thal-vex khal-en-ak-ven.

Vex-Mor — life-moon dragonfly — water-SUP live-PAST-DIRECT

Vex-Mor — the purple dragonflies — danced above the water.

IV. The Crystal

Ven-Khal-sha Ven-Khal-crystal-thar khal-vex khal-ak-ven.

Ven-Khal-ERG Ven-Khal-crystal-ACC hand-IN do-PAST-DIRECT

Ven-Khal held the Ven-Khal crystal in his hand.

"Khal-en," Ven-Khal-sha en-ak-ven.

"Ready," Ven-Khal-ERG say-PAST-DIRECT

"I am ready," he said aloud.

Khal-thex-sha mor-khal-en-ak-ven. Ghor-ven: 58… 55… 52. Thal-ven: 44… 40… 36.

Life-beat-ERG NEG-do-PAST-DIRECT. Chest-DAT: 58… 55… 52. Abdomen-DAT: 44… 40… 36.

The heartbeats slowed: 58… 55… 52. 44… 40… 36.

V. The Last Words

"Xha'ruun-sha the-thar khal-en-ven," Ven-Khal-sha en-ak-ven.

"People-ERG earth-ACC do-PRES-DIRECT," Ven-Khal-ERG say-PAST-DIRECT

"The people work the land," Ven-Khal whispered.

Ven-Khal-sha Ven-Khal-crystal-thar khal-tor en-ak-ven. Crystal-sha khar-ak-ven — xa-xa, sha-xa, thex-xa — Xha-Ven-ven thex-en-ak-ven.

Ven-Khal-ERG Ven-Khal-crystal-ACC hand-ABL release-PAST-DIRECT. Crystal-ERG flash-PAST-DIRECT — all-knowledge, all-words, all-memories — Xha-Ven-DAT carry-PAST-DIRECT

Ven-Khal released the crystal. It flashed once — all knowledge, all words, all memories — transmitted to Xha-Ven.

"Xa-xa the-rass en-ak-ven."

"All-all earth-unity be-PAST-DIRECT"

"Everything is connected to everything."

Khal-thex-sha en-mor-ak-ven.

Life-beat-ERG be-NEG-PAST-DIRECT

The hearts were still.

VI. The Return

Ven-Khal-thar the-khal-vex khal-ak-ven — Thar-thar-vex thex-ven en-ak-ven.

Ven-Khal-ACC earth-life-SUP do-PAST-DIRECT — Thar-valley-SUP hill-DAT be-PAST-DIRECT

They returned Ven-Khal to the soil — upon the hill above the Valley of Thar.

Ven-Shar thex-ven thex-khal-ak-ven. 200-gharr mor-ven khal-ak en-mor-ven.

Ven-Shar tree-DAT plant-PAST-DIRECT. 200-cycle time-DAT forest be-FUT-DIRECT

They planted a young Ven-Shar. In 200 cycles, it will be a forest.

Ruun-sha the-thar khal-en-ven. Ven-Khal the-ven thex-ak-ven.

People-ERG earth-ACC do-PRES-DIRECT. Ven-Khal earth-DAT return-PAST-DIRECT

The people work the earth. Ven-Khal has returned to the earth.
