Sha-En Ven-El

The First Word

El the-vex the-ven en-ak-ven.
She clay-wall-at sit-be-PAST-DIRECT
She sat at the clay wall.

El khal-thar sha-ven en-ak-ven.
She fingers-ACC look be-PAST-DIRECT
She looked at her fingers.

Khal-mor the-vex thar-el en-ak-ven.
Hand-from clay-ON mark-PL be-PAST-DIRECT
Her fingers left marks on the wet clay.

El sha-ven en-ak-ven: "The-ven sha-en-ven."
She understand be-PAST-DIRECT: "This word-is."
She understood: "This is a word."
