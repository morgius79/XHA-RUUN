Gharra Rass

The Deep Covenant — 𐤑𐰀𐰺𐰆𐰣

Moryn-ak gharra-the en-ak-ven.

Moryn-PL depth-INESSIVE be-PAST-DIR

The Moryn dwell in the depths.

Ven-Khal-sha el-ak-rass sha-ak-ven.

Ven-Khal-ERG they-PL-COM speak-PAST-DIR

Ven-Khal spoke with them.

Moryn-sha ven-ak-ven: "The khal-en-ven?"

Moryn-ERG say-PAST-DIR: "You want-PRES-DIR?"

The Moryn said: "What do you want?"

Ven-Khal-sha en-ak-ven: "Sha-ak rass-ven the-ven khal-en-ven."

Ven-Khal-ERG say-PAST-DIR: "We-PL unity-DAT word-DAT want-PRES-DIR"

Ven-Khal said: "We seek unity."

Moryn-sha khar-ak-ven — rass-mor el-ak-tor en-ak-ven.

Moryn-ERG shine-PAST-DIR — silence they-PL-ABL be-PAST-INFR

The Moryn glowed — silence came from them.

"Sha-ak the-rass khal-en-mor," Moryn-sha en-ak-ven.

"We-PL you-COM make-FUT-INFR," Moryn-ERG say-PAST-DIR

"We will make a covenant with you," said the Moryn.
