Chapter 31: Future of Théxar

"The planet does not stand still. In a hundred thousand cycles, Kharak will shift 2° north. In a million — a new volcano will be born. In a billion — life will become unrecognizable. We are but an instant in its eyes." — From "Forecasts of the Guild of Scientists," +4 000 E.U.

31.1.1 Magnetic Reversal

The last reversal was ~28 000 cycles ago. The average interval is 350 000 cycles. The reversal is already "overdue" — possibly the current epoch (Brunhes-Théxar) is one of the longest in the planet's history.

31.3 Anthropogenic Influence

Factor Level

31.3.2 Forecasts

At the current level of impact, the civilization will not cause climatic changes comparable to natural cycles within the next 10 000 cycles.
