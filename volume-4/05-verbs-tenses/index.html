Volume IV: Language — Chapter 5: Verbs and Tense

"The verb is the engine of the sentence. It carries action, time, and source of information. Without a verb, there is no utterance." — Grammar of Xha'Ruun, Academy

Related sections: → see [Volume IV, Ch.3, "Grammar"] Length: 25 pages

5.1 Conjugation

Xha'Ruun verbs conjugate for:

  • Tense (4)
  • Number (3)
  • Evidentiality (4 — mandatory)

5.2 Tense System

Tense Suffix Example Translation
Past -ak khal-ak did
Present -en khal-en does
Future -mor khal-mor will do
Gnomic -el khal-el does (always)

5.3 Example

Xha'ruun-sha the-thar khal-en-ven
People-ERG soil-ACC do-NPST-DIR
"The people cultivate the soil (I see it)"
